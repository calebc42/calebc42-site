## Key Accomplishments {#key-accomplishments}


### Team Leadership and Staff Management {#team-leadership-and-staff-management}

-   **Situation:** Effective team leadership was crucial for maintaining a smooth workflow during demanding shifts.
-   **Task:** To lead and motivate the kitchen crew, assign team members to workstations, and manage breaks.
-   **Action:** Assigned team members to workstations based on skills and strategically managed the break schedule.
-   **Result:** Maintained optimal staffing levels throughout every shift, ensuring continuous operational efficiency.


### Kitchen Workflow and Throughput Optimization {#kitchen-workflow-and-throughput-optimization}

-   **Situation:** The "Expediter" station acts as the central control point for the entire kitchen.
-   **Task:** To function as the lead Expediter, coordinating the entire kitchen workflow.
-   **Action:** Managed the complete order lifecycle by prioritizing orders and coordinating cook times between stations.
-   **Result:** Increased kitchen throughput and order accuracy by preventing bottlenecks and reducing mistakes.


### Financial Closing and Data Management {#financial-closing-and-data-management}

-   **Situation:** Precise daily financial closing and data management were critical for business oversight.
-   **Task:** To manage all daily financial closing procedures, including deposits and sales data.
-   **Action:** Executed daily financial closing procedures by recording data, handling deposits, and computing daily sales data into Excel.
-   **Result:** Ensured accurate and timely daily financial reporting for a high-revenue business.


### Data-Driven Inventory Control and Forecasting {#data-driven-inventory-control-and-forecasting}

-   **Situation:** Effective inventory management was essential to control food costs and prevent waste.
-   **Task:** To oversee inventory management, including tracking levels and forecasting demand.
-   **Action:** Monitored inventory levels in the Radiant database and utilized sales data to accurately forecast demand.
-   **Result:** Improved inventory accuracy, leading to a significant decrease in stock-outs and food waste.


### Labor Cost Control and KPI Achievement {#labor-cost-control-and-kpi-achievement}

-   **Situation:** Keeping labor costs within the corporate KPI was a primary responsibility.
-   **Task:** To consistently maintain labor costs under the KPI of 22% of total daily revenue.
-   **Action:** Implemented strategic staff and break management, aligning labor hours directly with fluctuating customer traffic.
-   **Result:** Consistently achieved the 22% labor cost KPI, contributing to profitability.


### Leadership Recognition and Promotion {#leadership-recognition-and-promotion}

-   **Situation:** Opportunity for advancement arose.
-   **Task:** To meet requirements for a leadership position.
-   **Action:** Successfully completed and received all required internal certifications.
-   **Result:** Earned a formal promotion to Shift Lead.
